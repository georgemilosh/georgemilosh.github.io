I will be teaching at the [ASAP Summer School on Machine Learning for Space 2026](https://asap-space.eu/asap-summer-school), which will take place **8-12 June 2026** at **KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden**.

![ASAP project logo](/assets/img/ASAP.png)

The summer school is aimed at graduate students and early-career researchers interested in heliophysics, magnetospheric science, space weather, and scientific machine learning. The program combines introductory lectures with hands-on coding sessions, covering topics such as data access and preprocessing, supervised and unsupervised learning, dimensionality reduction, classification, prediction, and event detection using mission data.

Participants will work with Python-based tools including scikit-learn, Pandas, Jupyter, and PyTorch, and the week will conclude with lightning talks and discussion sessions. Participation is free of charge, but registration is required and travel, accommodation, and meals are not covered.

More details, the preliminary schedule, and the registration information are available on the [official summer school page](https://asap-space.eu/asap-summer-school).
